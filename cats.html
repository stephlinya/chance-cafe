Adoptable cats

Are you looking for a fluffy buddy for your family?

Take a look at our available cats. Our cafe is home to anywhere between 8 - 12 kitties. If you are interested in a kitty, please read our adoption requirements and process and fill out an adoption interest form. Note: Filling out an adoption interest form does not necessarily guarantee you will be able to adopt your kitty of interest. Our adoption coordinator will reach out to you in 2-3 business days. An adoption interview will be set up during this time. Please check your email for this information. These bundles of cuteness are looking for their fur-ever home.
